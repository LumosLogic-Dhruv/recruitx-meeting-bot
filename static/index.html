How to Use the System

Get up and running with RecruitX AI Interviewer in four easy steps.

1 Step 1: Custom Prompt Generation

Navigate to the Prompt Generator view. Type the name of the role (e.g. Senior Frontend Engineer) and click "Generate". The AI Prompt Engineer will leverage OpenAI to design a custom structured system prompt for this specific job role, defining candidate experience parameters, interview flow rules, and saving it automatically inside your cloud prompt library.

2 Step 2: Start an Interview Room Session

Go to the Interview Room. Paste your active Google Meet call URL (e.g., https://meet.google.com/abc-defg-hij). Choose the target prompt from the dropdown of saved role prompts, type the candidate's name, and click Start Interview.

3 Step 3: Monitor Live Conversations

The RecruitX bot joins the Google Meet call. Inside the call, it greets the candidate and starts interviewing. You'll see messages appear inside the Live Conversation Feed. Watch candidate responses and interviewer follow-up questions update in real-time.

4 Step 4: End the Session & Analyze Scorecards

When the interview wraps up, click End Interview. The system will disconnect the bot, compile the full transcript, and trigger an evaluation. Review the detailed scorecard containing a rating out of 10, key strengths, concerns, and suggested next steps.
